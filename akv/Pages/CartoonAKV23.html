Deni Cartoon of Voronsky, 1923
"Ecce Homo!" Cartoon of Voronsky on cross, under attack by arrows labelled: Tarasov-Rodionov, Rodov, Lelevich, and Volin (supporters of the journal, Na postu [On Guard]). Drawn by Deni, 1923, published in "Prozhektor," #20, p. 25.
