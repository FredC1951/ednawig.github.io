Letter from Military Collegium of the Supreme Court of the USSR, 27 March 1989
Description: Letter from the Military Collegium of the Supreme Court of the USSR on 27 March 1989, informing Voronsky's granddaughter that Voronsky had been sentenced to death on August 13, 1937, and probably shot on the same day.
